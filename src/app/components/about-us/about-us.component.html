MESSAGE FROM THE CEO

Mohammad Almatrafi

The United Cement Industrial Company (UCIC) is honored to have the support of the Government of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, having secured a concession building permit in Al Lith city to manufacture various types of cement that meet global standards through efficient fuel customization. Committed to our national duty, UCIC prioritizes Saudi citizens in all aspects of our operations, with nearly 100% of leadership positions held by qualified Saudi staff. Since our establishment in 2013, we have achieved platinum status and emphasize the importance of continuous training at local and global specialized centers. Aligned with Vision 2030, we focus on efficiency and competitiveness in our technical and administrative operations, striving to be a vital contributor to the Kingdom's development and prosperity.

Vision

Aiming to be the most efficient and effective cement producer in Saudi Arabia, we focus on optimizing operations and adopting innovative practices to enhance quality.

Mission

Creating additional values in Saudi Cement Industry through innovative and sustainable using of the natural resources in order to serve our society and our Customers
